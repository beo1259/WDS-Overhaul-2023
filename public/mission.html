Our Mission

INKLINK; A Link To the Future

Introduction

Welcome to InkLink, where our mission is to revolutionize accessibility in note-taking. Our CEO, Stephanie Wong, started InkLink with the vision of creating a note-taking application that caters to the diverse accessibility needs of people from various backgrounds.

The Drawing of INKLINK

Stephanie Wong's journey began as a high school student facing language barriers. This challenge sparked a passion for technology and entrepreneurship, leading to the creation of INKLINKâ€”an innovative app designed to make learning more accessible.

Accessibility and User-Friendliness

Accessibility and user-friendliness are the pillars of InkLink. We're committed to providing an intuitive interface that accommodates a wide range of disabilities, ensuring that everyone has access to a powerful learning tool.

Our Features

  • Speech-to-text functions
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Text-to-speech capabilities
  • Language translations
  • Captioning for audio and video notes

Design Philosophy

Our UI is crafted to be warm, inviting, and sleek. We emphasize a simple, singular color scheme, focusing on ease of use and removing barriers to access.

Inklings: Our Users

InkLink is dedicated to serving students with language barriers and disabilities. We strive to be a beacon of support for immigrants, refugees, and anyone in need of a helping hand in their educational journey.
